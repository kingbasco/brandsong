ABOUT US

Brandsong enhances your brand image and value with bespoke music.

BrandSong is a music and sound content creation business based in Nigeria. The company is focused on using data to optimize its services, and is positioned for growth in the music content creation market. BrandSong's vision is to become the go-to music and sound content creation company for Africa, and its mission is to consistently deliver accurate, relevant, and excellent outcomes for brands and businesses through the seamless combination of creativity and data.

BrandSong's core values include research and knowledge, partnership, uniqueness, and excellence. The company believes that every piece of data provides useful insight for decision making in the creative process, and it collaborates closely with clients and members of its creative collective to meet and exceed expectations. BrandSong is committed to providing bespoke and high value solutions for its clients, and produces world-class products through meticulous processes that pay attention to details.

BrandSong offers a variety of services to its clients, including theme songs, anthems, commercials, jingles, film scores, and soundtracks.
